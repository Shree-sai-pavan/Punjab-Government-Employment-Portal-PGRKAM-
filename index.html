Financial Support & Funding

Get financial assistance and funding support for entrepreneurs and small business owners

Key Highlights: Over ₹5000 Crore disbursed | 50,000+ Entrepreneurs Supported | 200+ Partner Banks

Access government-backed loans, subsidies, and grants through our comprehensive financial schemes designed for Punjab's entrepreneurs.

Mudra Loan Scheme

Up to ₹10 Lakhs

Eligibility: Small businesses, startups, entrepreneurs

Purpose: Business expansion, equipment purchase, working capital

Interest Rate: 8-12% per annum

  • Shishu: Up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: ₹50,000 to ₹5 Lakhs
  • Tarun: ₹5 Lakhs to ₹10 Lakhs

Detailed Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Business should be non-farm activity
  • No minimum educational qualification required
  • Should not be a defaulter in any financial institution
  • Business should have valid licenses and registrations

Application Process:

  1. Prepare business plan and financial projections
  2. Visit nearest bank branch or MUDRA portal
  3. Submit application with required documents
  4. Bank appraisal and credit assessment
  5. Loan sanction and disbursement within 7-10 days

Additional Benefits:

  • Moratorium period of 6 months for repayment
  • No collateral required for loans up to ₹10 lakhs
  • Flexible repayment tenure up to 5 years
  • Online application facility available

Success Metrics:

Over 30 million loans disbursed nationwide, with 85% success rate in business sustainability.

Contact Information:

MUDRA Helpline: 1800-180-1111
Email: mudra@sidbi.in
Website: www.mudra.org.in

Punjab Start-Up Fund

Up to ₹25 Lakhs

Eligibility: Tech startups, innovative business ideas

Purpose: Seed funding for new ventures

Benefits:

  • Equity-free funding
  • Mentorship support
  • Incubation facilities
  • Market linkage assistance

Detailed Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 18-40 years
  • Indian citizen with Punjab domicile
  • Innovative business idea with market potential
  • Should not be a defaulter in any financial institution
  • Business should be registered as Pvt Ltd or LLP

Application Process:

  1. Register on Punjab Startup Portal
  2. Submit detailed business plan and financial projections
  3. Present idea to evaluation committee
  4. Due diligence and legal compliance check
  5. Funding approval and disbursement within 30 days

Additional Benefits:

  • Access to co-working spaces at subsidized rates
  • Networking opportunities with investors
  • Legal and IP support
  • Fast-track approvals for government licenses

Success Metrics:

Funded 500+ startups with 75% survival rate, generating 10,000+ jobs and ₹500 crore in economic activity.

Contact Information:

Startup Helpline: 1800-180-START
Email: startups@punjab.gov.in
Website: startup.punjab.gov.in
